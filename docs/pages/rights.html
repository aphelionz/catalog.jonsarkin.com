Scholarly & Educational Use

We encourage the use of this catalog as a scholarly resource. The following uses are generally permitted without a formal license:

  • Citation — Catalog data (titles, dates, dimensions, provenance) may be cited with proper attribution to the Jon Sarkin Catalog Raisonné.
  • Thumbnail reproduction — Small-scale reproductions of catalog images may be used in the context of scholarly review, criticism, or classroom teaching, consistent with fair use principles. Always include the standard credit line (see below).
  • Linking — Direct links to individual catalog records are stable and may be freely shared.

If you are unsure whether your intended use qualifies, please contact us or reach out to ARS directly.

Publication & Reproduction

Reproduction of Sarkin's work in print or digital publications—including books, exhibition catalogs, periodicals, websites, and broadcast media—requires a license from the Artists Rights Society (ARS). This applies to:

  • Full-size or cropped reproductions in any publication
  • Use in exhibition materials (catalogs, wall text reproductions, promotional materials)
  • Commercial use of any kind (merchandise, advertising, products)
  • Film, television, and documentary use

High-Resolution Images

High-resolution images for approved reproduction purposes are available through the estate. Image requests should accompany or follow an approved ARS license. Please include the catalog number(s) of the work(s) you wish to reproduce when making your request.

Images provided for reproduction are for one-time use only unless otherwise specified in the license agreement.

Credit Line

All reproductions of Sarkin's work must include the following credit line:

Jon Sarkin, [Title], [Date]. [Medium]. [Dimensions]. [Collection/Owner]. © The Jon Sarkin Estate / Artists Rights Society (ARS), New York.

For example:

Jon Sarkin, Untitled (Fish and Eyes), c. 2008. Marker and ink on paper. 12 × 12 in. (30.5 × 30.5 cm). Private collection. © The Jon Sarkin Estate / Artists Rights Society (ARS), New York.
